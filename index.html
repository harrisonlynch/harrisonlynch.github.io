Harrison Lynch

Hi, I’m Harrison Lynch. I’m an associate research principal at Accenture Labs. My time there is primarily spent on researching, writing, and marketing the Accenture Technology Vision—Accenture’s 3 to 5 year outlook on technology. Outside of the Vision, I’ve worked on reports concerning the future of work, accessible technologies, and data ethics.

Before moving to the Bay Area, I studied Industrial Engineering at the University of Pittsburgh.

I enjoy writing pieces that speak to technical and non-technical audiences alike.
